Call for Contribution

The European network Heloise was launched in May 2012. This network seeks to foster scientific meeting about the social history of universities in Europe since the Middle Ages, while reflecting on the implementation of technical tools and solutions for effective collaboration in the production, processing and provision of digital data and archives.

The 9th Heloise Workshop deals with research on online databases in the academic field of history. It gathers researchers from the Humanities, Computer Science and Digital Humanities who are using Semantic Web technologies and are concerned with Ontology building in their projects.

As part of the DFG-Project Early Modern Professorial Career Patterns Methodological research on online databases of academic history we are able to provide accommodation and the additional scientific collaboration event funded by DFG.
